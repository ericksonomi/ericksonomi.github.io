Mesh Integration

The Mesh Integration system is an advanced technique used to add volume or apply hair extensions, ideal for individuals with alopecia, thinning hair, or areas with low hair density. This method is known for providing a natural appearance and its versatility, as it can be tailored to various needs and styles.

Steps of the Mesh Integration System:

1. Hair and Scalp Assessment: Before starting, a thorough evaluation of the client's hair and scalp is conducted. This includes identifying areas with hair loss or low density and determining the best approach for integration.

2. Selection of the Mesh: A fine and flexible mesh or net is selected, which will serve as the base for integrating the additional hair. This mesh is lightweight and breathable, designed to be comfortable and non-irritating to the scalp.

3. Customization of the Mesh: The mesh is cut and shaped to fit the specific size and shape of the area that needs coverage. This process is crucial to ensure the mesh fits perfectly on the client’s scalp.

4. Securing the Mesh: The mesh is placed and securely attached to the client's existing hair using small micro-rings, clips, or stitching. This attachment is gentle and does not damage the natural hair.

5. Hair Integration: Hair extensions that match the client’s natural hair color, texture, and density are selected. These extensions are integrated into the mesh using stitching or knotting techniques, creating a natural look that blends seamlessly with the client’s existing hair.

6. Cut and Style: Once the extensions are fully integrated, the hair is cut and styled to achieve the desired look. This step is essential to ensure the added hair blends naturally with the client’s hair and to create a flawless finish.

7. Maintenance and Care: The Mesh Integration system requires regular maintenance to ensure the mesh and extensions remain in good condition. This maintenance may include adjusting the attachment, cleaning, and trimming the hair. It is recommended that clients return to the salon every 4 to 6 weeks for this upkeep.

Benefits of the Mesh Integration System

Natural Appearance: This system allows for the creation of volume and hair density that looks and feels natural.

Customization: The mesh and extensions are customized to fit the client’s individual needs perfectly.

Versatility: It can be used both to add volume and to cover areas of alopecia or low density and is suitable for both men and women.

Non-Invasive: Unlike other hair restoration methods, this system does not require surgery and is a less aggressive option.

The Mesh Integration system is an excellent choice for those seeking an effective aesthetic solution for thinning hair or hair loss, providing results that boost confidence and significantly improve the appearance of the hair.

Hair Loss

Hair loss, or alopecia, is a widespread issue affecting millions of people globally. For women, this condition often results in thinning hair on the crown, posing challenges that go beyond aesthetics, impacting confidence and emotional well-being. Contributing factors range from genetics to hormonal changes and underlying health conditions, making each case unique.

Treatment options are varied, including topical applications like minoxidil, medical therapies, and cosmetic alternatives such as custom wigs and hairpieces. At Total Secret Hair Club, we provide personalized solutions that blend seamlessly with natural hair, offering a discreet and effective way to regain your appearance and self-assurance.
